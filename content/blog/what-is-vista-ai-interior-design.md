# What is Vista? AI Interior Design Explained

Vista is an AI-powered interior design app built by [Tunzone](https://tunzone.com). It lets anyone redesign their living space in minutes — no designer appointment, no expensive consultations, no waiting weeks for mood boards.

## How It Works

1. **Upload a room photo** — Take a picture of your room with your phone or upload from your gallery.
2. **Describe your style** — Tell Vista what you want: "warm minimalist living room with natural wood" or "modern Scandinavian bedroom."
3. **Get a photorealistic redesign** — Vista's AI generates a realistic image of your room with new furniture and finishes.

## Real Furniture, Not AI Imagination

Unlike most AI room design tools, Vista doesn't invent fictional products. Every piece of furniture in your design is **real** — sourced from Armenian marketplace retailers with direct links to buy. Sofas, tables, chairs, lighting, and decor are all available for purchase.

## Two Design Modes

- **Quick Room** — Redesign a single room from one photo. Takes about 18 minutes and costs 10 tokens.
- **Full Project** — Upload your floor plan and redesign every room in your apartment with a coordinated design. Starts at 40 tokens.

## Who Is Vista For?

Vista is for anyone who wants to:

- See how their room would look with different furniture before buying
- Get interior design inspiration grounded in real, purchasable products
- Redesign their space affordably without hiring a professional designer
- Actually buy the furniture shown in their AI-generated design
