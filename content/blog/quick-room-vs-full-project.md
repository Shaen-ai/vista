# Quick Room vs Full Project — Which Vista Mode Should You Choose?

Vista offers two ways to redesign your space. Here's how to pick the right one.

## Quick Room

**Best for:** Redesigning a single room quickly.

- Upload one room photo
- Get a complete AI redesign with matched catalog furniture
- Takes about 18 minutes
- Costs 10 tokens (~400 AMD)
- Edit with chat after generation

Quick Room is perfect when you want to refresh one space — a living room, bedroom, or home office — and see immediate results with purchasable furniture.

## Full Project

**Best for:** Redesigning your whole apartment with a coordinated look.

- Upload a floor plan and room photos
- Vista analyzes the layout and detects all rooms
- Creates a coordinated design concept across every room
- Generates renders, technical plans, and a PDF package
- Starts at 40 tokens for 5+ rooms

Full Project is the choice when you're moving into a new place, renovating, or want every room to feel like part of the same design language.

## Quick Comparison

| | Quick Room | Full Project |
|---|---|---|
| Input | 1 room photo | Floor plan + room photos |
| Output | 1 redesigned room render | Multi-room renders + plans + PDF |
| Time | ~18 minutes | Varies by room count |
| Cost | 10 tokens | 40+ tokens |
| Best for | Single room refresh | Whole-home redesign |
